## Vision

ARGUS is an AI-powered quantitative research terminal designed to combine:

* Market Data
* Quantitative Analytics
* Portfolio Intelligence
* Multi-Agent Research
* Document Intelligence

into a single research platform.

Instead of functioning as a chatbot wrapped around market data, ARGUS follows a layered architecture where deterministic financial analytics, retrieval systems, and specialized agents collaborate to generate grounded research outputs.

The long-term objective is to build a modern research terminal inspired by:

* Bloomberg Terminal
* BlackRock Aladdin
* TradingView
* Institutional Quant Platforms

while remaining explainable, transparent, and engineer-friendly.

## Quant Engine

ARGUS computes all metrics mathematically.

No metric is generated by an LLM.

Implemented:

* Annual Return
* Volatility
* Sharpe Ratio
* Beta
* Maximum Drawdown

### Design Principles

* Deterministic
* Reproducible
* Testable
* Explainable

---

## Portfolio Analytics

## Multi-Agent Intelligence Layer

ARGUS uses specialized research agents instead of a single generic assistant.

### Market Agent

Analyzes:

* Price
* Trend
* Volatility
* Performance

Produces:

* Market Summary

### Risk Agent

Analyzes:

* Beta
* Drawdown
* Volatility
* Sharpe Ratio

Produces:

* Risk Assessment

### News Agent

Analyzes:

* Company News
* Market News
* Headlines

Produces:

* News Summary

### Strategy Agent

Consumes outputs from all other agents.

Produces:

* Bull Case
* Bear Case
* Neutral Case
* Risks
* Opportunities

## Knowledge Layer (RAG)

Agents can retrieve evidence from:

* 10-K Filings
* 10-Q Filings
* Annual Reports
* Earnings Calls
